Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Sola for?

Sola is designed for undergraduate students, graduate students, postdocs, and early career researchers looking to find the right research opportunities. It's also valuable for lab directors and PIs who want to connect with students who are passionate about their specific research areas.

How does semantic search work?

Unlike traditional keyword search, our semantic search understands the meaning behind research concepts. It uses advanced natural language processing to match you with labs and papers based on conceptual similarity, not just literal word matches.

When will Sola launch?

We're currently in private beta with select research institutions. Join our waitlist to be notified when we open up access more broadly, expected in late 2025.

Is Sola free to use?

Sola offers a free tier for students and researchers with basic features. Premium features, like advanced analytics and priority lab introductions, will be available through a subscription model.
